Programs & Tracks

Web / App Basics

HTML/CSS basics, AppSheet intro, simple automations, GitHub Pages hosting.

Branding & Digital Design

Business Support

Docs & templates, presentation skills, basic client communication workflow.

Eligibility

  • Polytechnic / UG students (any year), basic computer proficiency
  • Learning attitude; weekly check-ins और task submissions
  • Orientation के बाद agreement acceptance

Process (STEP 1–5)

Step 1: Application Submission (1 Day)

Google Form भरें और basic details submit करें।

Step 2: Screening & Selection (2 Days)

Profile review + short interaction/assignment (यदि आवश्यक हो)।

Step 3: Orientation & Agreement (3 Days)

Orientation call + guidelines + internship agreement.

Step 4: Internship Commencement (4 Days)

Tasks allocation, weekly progress reporting, mentor support.

Step 5: Project Submission & Certification

Final submission, feedback, और certificate issuance + verification.
